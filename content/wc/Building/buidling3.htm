Building Community
In 1975, Women from all over the Yukon celebrated International Woman’s Year by creating five large needlepoint wall hangings that celebrated women in the north. These artworks have been displayed in the Members Lounge of the Yukon Government building since the building’s official opening in May 1976. The panels depict the seasons of the north including a fifth season, “Survival,” the cold gray days between winter and spring and between fall and winter.

Members of the Whitehorse Business and Professional Women’s Club stitch one of the tapestry panels.
