Imperial Order of the Daughters of the Empire

The Imperial Order of the Daughters of the Empire (IODE) was founded in 1900 in Montreal during the Boer War and quickly grew into the largest women's patriotic group in the British Empire. Its members found many ways to serve their communities and country, particularly during the two World Wars.

Women of the Dawson City chapter gather for a reception in honour of Mrs. George Black, July 29, 1922.

Girl Guides of Canada

The Girl Guides of Canada, an organization that encourages independence, leadership and public service in young women, has been active in the Yukon since 1914.
