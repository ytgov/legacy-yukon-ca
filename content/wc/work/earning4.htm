Yukon Women Work Hard
 
“I born in Eagle, Alaska and raise there and my parents they move to Old Crow around 1940. I was 16 years old when I got to Old Crow, by that time people don’t know about where is Old Crow. Since I start write news for Old Crow so people start to know about where to go, and they really help their people and their community.”

Journalist Edith Josie prepares her weekly newspaper column. For over 40 years, Miss Josie’s accounts of the lives and doings of the people of Old Crow have attracted attention from all over the world. Although she writes in English, her phrasing is Gwich’in. Her work has been honoured with a heritage award from the Yukon Historical & Museums Association and a National Aboriginal Achievement Award.
