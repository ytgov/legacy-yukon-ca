Sternwheelers of the Yukon is presented by the Yukon Archives in Whitehorse, Yukon. The Yukon Archives is part of the Cultural Services Branch of the Department of Tourism and Culture, Government of Yukon.

The content for this web exhibit is based on a three panel display entitled Sternwheelers which was produced in 2005 by the Yukon Archives.
