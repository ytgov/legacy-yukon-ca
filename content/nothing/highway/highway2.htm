The Alaska Highway

The construction of the Alaska Highway caused great displacement but also brought new opportunities for First Nations people. Some of them guided surveyors through the country, others worked at the highway camps. Kitty Smith was able to buy her family a truck with the money she earned from selling handmade clothing.
