Politics

As well as governing their own communities, First Nations people are now active in almost every aspect of government: national, territorial, municipal and First Nations.

Sam Johnston was one of the Yukon chiefs who presented Together Today for Our Children Tomorrow to the Prime Minister of Canada. Mr. Johnston was also the first aboriginal person to be named Speaker of a legislature in Canada, serving the Yukon Legislative Assembly as Speaker from 1985 to 1992.

In 1989, Margaret Commodore became the first native woman Minister of Justice in Canada.
